Product Story

Made to look and feel like a game, not a worksheet.

Players take the role of a wizard fighting Pi creatures in a world where mathematics powers every spell. The learning path is embedded inside combat, not placed beside it.

Early stages focus on confidence and rhythm. Later content escalates through fractions, decimals, equations, functions, and trigonometry as players unlock stronger magic.
